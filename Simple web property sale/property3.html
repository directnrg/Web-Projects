HIGHLIGHTS

Walk into this stunning, classic Art Deco building & take the elevator to #405. High ceilings & hardwood floors create a sense of elegance, while archways allow light to spill from room to room. The large living & quaint dining rooms are adjacent to remodeled kitchen, perfect for entertaining. Attentive remodel includes recessed lighting, double-pane bay windows while honoring the moldings & decorative details that give this condo its SF character. Remodeled bath takes after the Art Deco surroundings but with a thoroughly modern sensibility. Walk-in closet off the spacious bedroom finishes off the classy interior. With abundance of natural light this corner unit has partial Views of Bay, 1 car parking, & storage. With GGB, Transamerica, & City Views from common roof deck, this well-established building boasts a prime location. Sharing a neighborhood close to Lafayette Park, famous landmarks, high-class restaurants & stately homes lend to a truly classic San Francisco living experience
