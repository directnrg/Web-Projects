HIGHLIGHTS

Built in 2005, this beautiful Craftsman home is in the heart of the Redwood Village. A spacious 4 bedroom 3 full bath offering a desirable open floor plan in an incredible Marin location. Formal living room with vaulted ceilings, family room with gas fireplace off large eat-in kitchen. The expansive master suite with ensuite bath features a soaking tub, separate glass shower enclosure and walk in closet dressing room. Private rear patio detailed with beautiful yard and garden accents and just one block to large park, playground, and basketball court. This pristine home is just minutes from downtown San Rafael, shops, farmer’s market and the open space trail heads. Close to public transit and easy commute to San Francisco to the south and Sonoma wine county to the north.This is truly a destination property!
